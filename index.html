    I'm Brian. Here's my story. I grew up in Long Island. Instead of playing lacrosse or enjoying the beach, I became a competitive gamer. Throughout my time gaming and competing, I've learned what it means to fall short after giving it everything.

    During college, I co-founded Grabbit. Grabbit was a peer-to-peer delivery company that leveraged existing retail customers by notifying their social networks. Friends of the existing retail customers were able to place orders for pickup by the retail customer. The existing retail customer would receive a discount for bringing new business to the retail store.

    I've started Grabbit because I believe we can leverage technology to enhance physical relationships. We increased the amount of times friends ate meals together, and the affinity towards one another increased even though they had self-intristic motives.

    Blockchain will re-shape the way society is organized due to new incentive structures. I'm constantly researching and writing about new products that will bring cryptocurrency to the masses, mainly focusing on product, UX, and marketing.

    I'm always looking for new opportuntiies in the blockchain space, so if you want to talk or just brainstorm ideas feel free to reach out!
