Hey, welcome to my site.

I'm Brian. I grew up in Long Island in the suburbs. I spent most of my childhood playing multiplayer games online because I was infatuated with how communities formed online. I was eventually drawn into the e-sports scene in high-school and I had a dream of becoming a professional player in Counterstrike.

My dreams fell short. I went on to college trying to find my next big dream. While traveling abroad, I found it. I fell in love with entrepreneurship and startups. There was a passion some people had for changing the world with a vision. It was infectious. I wanted to be a part of it.

During college, I co-founded Grabbit. I had this vision to enhance physical relationships between friends by leveraging an activity that they did already - food delivery. When users were at a physical location, they could ping out their location to friends and friends could place an order to the store. The user at the location would receive a discount for bringing in new business to the store. The economic incentives between the retailer, user, and friend were aligned.

That's how I found my love for blockchain. Gaming. Economic incentives between networks. It clicked for me - I saw how my history connected with crypto. I went all in, trying to soak up as much information as possible. I finally found something that gets me up in the morning and doesn't put me to bed.

I'm constantly researching and writing about new products that will bring cryptocurrency to the masses, mainly focusing on product, UX, and marketing. I'm looking for new opportunities in startups or blockchain, so if you want to talk or just brainstorm ideas feel free to reach out!
